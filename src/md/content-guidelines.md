# Content Guidelines

## Tone & Voice
- Sophisticated, professional, yet bold.
- Focus on quality, craftsmanship, and technology.
- Highlight sustainability commitments in a transparent, measurable way.

## Asset Guidelines
- Images must have dynamic, premium color grading.
- Icons should follow a minimalist, thin-stroke Lucide-React pattern.
- Clear and bold layouts with generous whitespace to match editorial designs.
