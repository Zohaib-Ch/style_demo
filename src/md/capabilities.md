# Capabilities System Guidelines

## Structure
Split capabilities into three distinct sectors:
1. **Fabric**: Advanced weaving, high-tech knitting, premium dyeing & finishing.
2. **Apparel**: World-class design, modern stitching facilities, sustainability-focused assembly.
3. **ICT**: Internet, communications, and technology innovations facilitating supply-chain excellence.

## Visual presentation
- High-definition image reveals.
- Hover-triggered interactive previews.
- Deep transitions with horizontal panels showing capabilities info.
