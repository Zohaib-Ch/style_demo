# Style Website Context and Architecture

## Brand Positioning
Style is a global leader in high-end, premium textile, fabric, apparel, and ICT manufacturing. The digital experience must feel premium, award-winning, tactile, and highly fluid.

## Technical Foundation
- Next.js 15 App Router
- TailwindCSS v4 for advanced styling and customization
- GSAP + ScrollTrigger for high-performance timeline and scroll animations
- Lenis for silky smooth momentum scrolling
- Framer Motion for micro-interactions, page transitions, and simple layout animations
